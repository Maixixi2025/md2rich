Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

Short version: We don't collect anything. There is no analytics, no cookies, no server-side processing. Your text stays in your browser.

1. Information We Collect

None. md2rich is a static client-side application. When you use it, no data is sent to our servers (we have no servers that receive your content). We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other tracking tool.

2. Third-Party Services

md2rich loads the following resources from public CDNs:

  • cdn.jsdelivr.net — JavaScript libraries (marked.js, DOMPurify, EasyMDE)
  • cdn.jsdelivr.net — Tailwind CSS

These CDNs may log your IP address as part of normal CDN operation. This is outside our control.

3. Cookies

md2rich sets no cookies. We do not use any cookie-based tracking or analytics.

4. Local Storage

md2rich may optionally use your browser's localStorage to remember your last editor content between visits. This data never leaves your device.

5. Advertising

If/when ads are added (likely Google AdSense), those ad networks may set their own cookies. You can opt out via your browser's privacy settings or via ad network opt-outs.

6. Contact

Privacy questions? Open an issue on GitHub.
